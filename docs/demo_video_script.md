# Nyaya-Setu: Demo Video Script (ElevenLabs Ready)

**Estimated Duration:** ~1:45 to 2:00 Minutes  
**Target Voice:** Professional, assuring, clear, and confident (Ideal for ElevenLabs AI Voice generation).

---

### [0:00 - 0:15] Introduction & The Problem
**[Visuals on Screen:]** 
Start with the sleek Nyaya-Setu Landing Page. Emphasize the taglines describing the "Autonomous Public Defender."

**Voiceover Script:**
"Welcome to Nyaya-Setu, the Autonomous Public Defender. Millions of citizens face legal issues but struggle with complex legal language and expensive fees. Nyaya-Setu bridges this gap by bringing an intelligent legal advocate directly to everyone’s fingertips."

---

### [0:15 - 0:35] Dashboard Overview
**[Visuals on Screen:]** 
Transition to the user logging in and viewing the Main Dashboard. Briefly show case statistics or the active cases timeline.

**Voiceover Script:**
"Let’s see how it works. Once a user securely logs in, they are greeted by their Dashboard. This space centralizes all their legal filings, tracking real-time status and maintaining a clear timeline of actions. To report a new incident, the user simply starts a new case."

---

### [0:35 - 1:10] The Multi-Agent Chat Experience (Intake & Interpretation)
**[Visuals on Screen:]** 
Show the Chat Interface. Display an **already completed chat session** where the user has described an incident (e.g., "My bike was stolen outside the market yesterday.") and the AI has responded with the legal breakdown.

**Voiceover Script:**
"Here is where the real magic happens, powered by our multi-agent architecture. In this existing chat session, you can see the user explained their issue in simple, everyday language. Our first agent—the 'Interpreter'—immediately went to work. Using advanced retrieval techniques, it instantly analyzed the incident, identified the exact Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (or BNS) sections violated, and explained them back clearly."

---

### [1:10 - 1:35] The Drafting Workspace
**[Visuals on Screen:]** 
The user clicks or transitions from the chat to the "Drafting Workspace", revealing a beautifully formatted, court-ready FIR or Legal Complaint in a PDF viewer.

**Voiceover Script:**
"With the facts gathered, our second agent—the 'Drafter'—seamlessly takes over. Using Gemini AI, it transforms the conversational chat into a formal, court-ready legal draft mapped perfectly to BNS laws. The user doesn't need to know legal formatting; they just review the completed case file and download the PDF."

---

### [1:35 - 2:00] Advisory Nudges, Features & Conclusion
**[Visuals on Screen:]** 
Switch back to the Dashboard highlighting the "Next Nudge" or "Follow-up" timeline. Briefly flash the language selector (Hindi/English) and a quick glimpse of the Admin portal. End with the Nyaya-Setu logo.

**Voiceover Script:**
"But our job doesn't end at filing. Our third agent, the 'Advisor,' proactively monitors the case. If an authority hasn’t responded in seven days, it automatically sends follow-up nudges, ensuring justice isn't delayed. With built-in support for regional languages and an admin portal that continuously learns from new legal data—Nyaya-Setu isn't just an app. It's your tireless, intelligent bridge to justice."
